Roots

Price County was originated by an act of the State Legislature on March 3, 1879, from parts of Chippewa and Lincoln Counties. When it began Price county was linked to Taylor county for Judicial purposes.Sheriff Dennis Needham of Taylor County appointed Chris Dardis of Phillips as the 1st Deputy Sheriff. Ever since then Price county has kept on thriving and growing and being based on their love and power for the outdoors and nature.
