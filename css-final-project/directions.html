How to get there

To get to Privce county you first go through downtown Westbend and go past the new fleet farm until you get to the freeway. You then turn right after the truck stop and stay on for a while. You may then have to get off in fondulac for fuel and stretch your legs. Then get back on the freeway past Stevens Point which is now your halfway point. Finally keep going on the freeway until Meril and then you will see a sign a little before saying, Welcome to Price County and you have now made it choose your own path.
